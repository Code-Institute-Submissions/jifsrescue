Mission Statement


Jif's Rescue exists to promote the humane treatment of all animals, by providing care, companionship and education to give our furry freinds the life they deserve.


About Us


Since our founding in 1977, we have aimed to provide love, care and compassion to animals of all varieties big and small. We are a no kills center that is driven by our four key principles: unconditional love, holistic care, public education and community outreach.


We started as a small, family run centre with our first rescue being an abandoned Mum and puppies needing a lot of support to return to health. After successfully healing and rehoming the little family, we couldn't stop there! We started answering more local calls to help and since then have gone from strength to strength. Over the years we have rescued, healed and rehomed hundreds of animals in need.


While we started out just as a group of three volunteers, we now count ourselves in the dozens of full time staff and casual volunteers all dedicated to our rescues! We are delighted that no matter how numerous we become, the caring ethos for both humans and animals has stayed paramount.


What We Do


We provide personalised care and vetinary help for each of our guests and residents with plenty of interaction and socialisation. Adoption to a loving home is our preferred outcome but where this is not possible, we will provide a high standard of lifetime care in-house.


We also provide advice and resources to pet owners in need of assistance. Through dedicated educational programmes and community work such as public talks and school trip hosting, we aim to provide the public with the skills required to be the best owners they can be.


For the last ten years, we have also had a highly successful partnership with a local mental health charity. Service users have the opportunity spend time with our animals providing valuable socialisation for our residents in a mutaually beneficial way.
